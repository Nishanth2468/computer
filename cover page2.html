about golf

Golf, unlike most ball games, cannot and does not utilize a standardized playing area, and coping with the varied terrains encountered on different courses is a key part of the game.

golf points

    • golf rules
  1. Take your time, but not too much.
  2. Losing the game can be frustrating, but losing your cool is significantly worse. Watch where you stand.
  3. Silence is golden. As a courtesy, do not move or make any noise when a fellow player is up to putt.
  4. Respect the green.
